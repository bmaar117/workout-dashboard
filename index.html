Golf Performance
Mobility and drills for a stronger, consistent swing
Golf Mobility Circuit
Do 2–3× per week after your workout. Takes 5–8 minutes.
Key Lifts for Golf Power
These exercises directly transfer to your game
Cable Woodchoppers
Why: Directly mimics rotational power of a golf swing.
How: Ares cable at high position. Stand sideways. Pull diagonally from high to low. Rotate through core.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 12–15 each side
Explosive pull, controlled return
Face Pulls
Why: Builds rotator cuff — prevents most common golf injury.
How: Ares at forehead height. Pull rope to face, elbows wide and high.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 12–15 — do on every upper body day
Romanian Deadlift
Why: Hip hinge strength = power at impact.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 8–10
Driver Distance GoalWith 3–4 months of this program + golf mobility work, expect 10–20 yards added through better hip rotation and shoulder stability.
