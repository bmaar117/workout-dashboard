Golf Performance
Drills and mobility for a stronger swing
Golf Mobility Circuit
Do 2–3× per week after your workout. Takes 5–8 minutes.
Cable Woodchoppers — Golf Swing Power
Why it matters: Directly mimics golf swing rotation.
How: Ares cable at high position. Stand sideways. Pull handle diagonally from high to low. Rotate through core, hips stable.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 12–15 each side
Face Pulls — Shoulder Protection
Why it matters: Builds rotator cuff — prevents the most common golf injury.
How: Ares at forehead height. Pull rope to face, flare elbows wide. Squeeze shoulder blades.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 12–15
