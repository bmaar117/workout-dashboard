Golf Mobility Circuit
Do 2–3× per week, after your workout. Takes 5–8 minutes.
Key Lifts for Golf Power
These exercises directly transfer to your game
Cable Woodchoppers
Why it matters: Directly mimics the rotational power of a golf swing.
How: Set Ares cable at high position. Stand sideways, pull handle diagonally from high to low across your body. Keep hips stable and rotate through core.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 12–15 each side
Focus: explosive pull, controlled return
Face Pulls
Why it matters: Builds rotator cuff strength — prevents the most common golf injury.
How: Set Ares at forehead height. Pull rope toward face, flare elbows wide, squeeze shoulder blades.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 12–15
Do these on every upper body day
Romanian Deadlift
Why it matters: Hip hinge strength = power through the ball at impact.
How: Push hips back, lower bar along legs, squeeze glutes to return.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 8–10
Bulgarian Split Squat
Why it matters: Single-leg stability improves your golf stance and prevents hip sway.
Sets/Reps: 3 × 8–10 each leg
Driver Distance Goal With 3–4 months of this program + golf mobility work, expect 10–20 yards added to your driver through better hip rotation and shoulder stability.
